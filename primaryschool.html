Netball match between schools

The netball match between all the schools around Simpang Ampat, Perlis. Many schools were involved in this match. My school hosted this match. As far as I remember, our school played against SK Kampung Serdang and we won that match. The overall champion of this netball tournament was SK Sanglang.

Teacher's Day celebration in 2015

My school held a Teacher's Day celebration. Our class performed a special presentation for the occasion. We sang several songs themed around Teacher's Day, dedicated to our teachers. During the celebration, the teachers dressed up like students. My friends and I took a picture with our math teacher, who was dressed like a primary school student.

Independence Day celebration

My school organized an Independence Day celebration. Various activities were prepared for both students and teachers. At that time, I participated in a freedom ride where we decorated bicycles with Malaysian flags. Participants in this freedom ride cycled around nearby villages surrounding the school.

Reading competition

I participated in a reading competition organized by my school. During that event, I had to read and memorize a book in order to explain my understanding of it later. Prizes for the winners were distributed the next day. I managed to secure second place in the competition. The first place winner was actually a junior student below me in grade level.
