Scouts in high school

My uniformed body in high school was the Scouts. I participated in all the activities organized by the Scouts. The first picture is from Co-curriculum Day, which required all the uniformed bodies to cook. We, the Scouts, cooked lasagna and won first place that day. Additionally, the second picture shows the uniformed bodies camp that was held. We stayed overnight for a few days in the dormitory. Finally, I led the royal parade on the Excellence Awards Day.

While in the dormitory

I stayed in the dormitory for nearly six years from Form 1 to Form 5 because SPM at that time was postponed due to the spread of COVID-19. During this period, a dinner was organized with all the dormitory residents. The last picture taken was a farewell gesture with the dormitory supervisor.

Library prefect leadership camp

At that time, the Tuanku Syed Faizuddin Putra Library prefect leadership camp was held at UniMAP. I was the library prefect holding the position of secretary. We went there to learn about gathering necessary data using computers provided by them. Additionally, we also visited the library used by UniMAP students daily.

The last day of high school

The last exam on that day was Computer Science, which was the final exam for my SPM. All the pictures above are of my classmates, and the middle picture is of a female friend who took her final exam at that time. The SPM results were released online, and I'm grateful to have achieved 6As in the SPM exams.
